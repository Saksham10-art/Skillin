About Us

“This portal is developed under the PM Internship Scheme to ensure fair, transparent, and smart allocation of internships across India. Using AI-powered matchmaking, we connect students, universities, and employers on a single platform.

Our system not only matches candidates based on skills, qualifications, and preferences, but also ensures rural students, action by giving lakhs opportunities to underrepresented categories (SC/ST/OBC/EWS, women, divyang).

Through real-time dashboards and reports, the Ministry of Corporate Affairs can monitor allocations with complete transparency and accountability. Our goal is to empower and strengthen every student to get access to industry internships, and employers find the right talent at the right time.”
